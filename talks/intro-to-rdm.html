Research data

Research data management

Common metadata standards

  • Dublin Core
    • Minimal but widely applicable>
  • ISATab — Investigation, Study, Assay
    • Aimed at lab & computational science
  • DDI — Data Documentation Initiative
    • Aimed at social science
  • SDMX — Statistical Data and Metadata eXchange
    • Aimed at statistical analysis
