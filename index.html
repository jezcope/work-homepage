Home

I help people use technology to let them focus on what matters most. I am currently responsible for research data management at the University of Sheffield. This includes:

  • ORDA (Online Research Data), a platform for researchers to share their data with the world, powered by figshare
  • Guidance, advice and templates for research data management, including data management planning, looking after data, and preservation & sharing
  • Policy development
  • Advocacy and outreach activities, including presentations to departments, institutes and research groups
  • Consultancy and support on discipline-specific research data management projects

I'm based in the University Library, but work closely with colleagues in Research & Innovation Services (RIS) and Corporate Information & Computing Services (CICS). We also have ongoing projects with a number of groups, including Sheffield Research Software Engineering and Insigneo.
